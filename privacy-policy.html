Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Introduction

Welcome to Play It My Way. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable privacy laws.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be completely transparent about our data practices:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from users
  • No User Accounts: Our games do not require registration or user accounts
  • No Email Addresses: We do not collect email addresses or contact information
  • No Names: We do not collect names, ages, or any identifying information
  • No Location Data: We do not track or store location information
  • No Payment Information: All games are free and we do not process payments

3. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses minimal cookies for essential functionality:

  • Essential Cookies: Basic session cookies for website functionality
  • No Tracking Cookies: We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies
  • No Third-Party Cookies: We do not allow third-party cookies
  • Local Storage: We may use browser local storage for game progress (stored only on your device)

4. How Our Games Work

Our educational games operate entirely in your browser:

  • Client-Side Only: All game logic runs on your device
  • No Server Storage: Game progress is not sent to our servers
  • Local Game Data: Scores and progress are stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not sync or backup game data

5. Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services:

  • Hosting: Our website is hosted on standard web hosting services
  • No Analytics: We do not use Google Analytics or similar tracking services
  • No Advertising: We do not display advertisements or use ad networks
  • External Links: Our support link (Buy Me a Coffee) is external and has its own privacy policy

6. Children's Privacy

We take children's privacy very seriously:

  • COPPA Compliance: We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • Parental Consent: No parental consent is required as we don't collect personal data
  • Safe Environment: Our games are designed to be safe for children
  • No Data Collection: Children can play our games without any data being collected

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the following rights (though we don't collect personal data):

  • Right to Access: You can request information about any data we might have (we have none)
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of any data (not applicable)
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of any data (not applicable)
  • Right to Portability: You can request data transfer (not applicable)
  • Right to Object: You can object to data processing (not applicable)

8. Data Security

While we don't collect personal data, we maintain security measures:

  • HTTPS Encryption: Our website uses secure HTTPS connections
  • No Data Storage: We have no databases or servers storing user data
  • Regular Updates: We keep our website and security measures updated
  • Secure Hosting: We use reputable hosting services with security measures

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at info@playitmyway.com.
